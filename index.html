Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our workflow, project delivery timelines, and payment structures.

Are the prices truly fixed, or will they change?

Every quote given is 100% transparent and contractually fixed before any work begins. There are zero hidden fees or unexpected line-item invoices. Any additional custom requirements out of the original scope can be handled separately via add-ons at your preference.

How fast will my website be live?

Delivery parameters depend entirely on your project tier. A clean structural Static Website typically goes live within 3–5 days, a Dynamic CMS site takes 7–15 days, and custom dynamic React applications require 10–20 days depending on the structural complexity.

Are domain names and hosting architectures included?

Domain registration and technical hosting setups are listed separately as clear add-ons. This allows you full independent ownership over your accounts while we handle the configuration pipelines, linking, and deployment mechanics end-to-end.

What is the development and review process?

Our process flows smoothly: from initial discussion to a written fixed quote, followed by structured asset design phases. You will review interactive drafts along the pipeline, and absolutely nothing goes live on production without your explicit sign-off.

Can a website be updated easily after deployment?

Yes. If you choose our Dynamic configuration tier, a fully secure custom admin dashboard is deployed so you can manage layout logs, update text, and manipulate database collections seamlessly without writing a single line of code.
